This is a work-in-progress. All images, literature, and interactions are subject to change.

Garden of Mirrors is an aesthetic-driven non-linear story about introspective statues.

Images and passages are paired and presented in random order.

This is a collaborative work between human and AI.

Technology:

  • Literature: Chat GPT 3.5
  • Images: Stable Diffusion 1.5
  • Framework: Angular 16.2.4
  • Deployment: Vercel

Programming & prompts, concepts & design, and additional editing & sculpting by Nicholas R. Kolodziej.
